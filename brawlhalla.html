My Review
★★★☆☆
40.5 hrs played
Brawlhalla feels like a cartoon fever dream where little legends jump, punch, and yeet each other off cliffs — all wrapped in soft colors and smooth controls. I log in for “just one match,” then suddenly it’s 2 a.m. and I’ve mained someone I swore I hated. It’s chaotic, but in a comfy way.

Pros

  • Cute, clean visuals with fun, bouncy vibes
  • Easy to pick up, hard to stop playing
  • So many characters (even Shovel Knight and Kung Fu Panda, hello?)
  • Matches are short, so it's perfect for a “quick game” (but never actually quick)

Cons

  • Some players think "spam one button" is a valid personality
  • You’ll fall off the stage more than you’d like to admit
  • Ranked? Stressful. But pretty.
  • Not having all Legends unlocked makes me a little sad
