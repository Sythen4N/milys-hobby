My Review
★★★★☆
45.6 hrs played
Genshin Impact is like stepping into a painting where everyone’s hot, the birds judge you, and falling off cliffs is just part of the journey. I play it to relax, explore beautiful landscapes, and sometimes cry over primogem poverty. It’s peaceful until a boss one-shots me or my 50/50 loses again.

Pros

  • Stunning visuals & calming music (10/10 vibe for escaping real life)
  • Exploration that feeds my inner wanderer
  • Elemental combat = satisfying sparkle boom
  • Characters with actual lore and ✨drip✨

Cons

  • Gacha pain is very real (Diluc again? Okay.)
  • Resin system makes me log in, fight 3 slimes, then log out
  • Story updates come slower than my motivation to farm artifacts
  • Endgame content is either Spiral Abyss... or emotional damage
