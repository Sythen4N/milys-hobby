My Review
★★★★☆
41.2 hrs played
Minecraft is that cute little game where everything’s made of blocks and nothing can hurt you... until it does. I boot it up for some “peaceful building” and next thing I know, I’m fighting for my life because I forgot to bring a sword... again. It’s cozy, it’s creative, and it’s full of moments where you ask yourself, “Why did I dig straight down?”

Pros

  • Infinite creativity—build anything, even secret underground noodle farms
  • Nostalgic soundtrack perfect for late-night vibing
  • Mods and shaders = ✨aesthetic overload✨
  • Multiplayer chaos with friends = friendship-ruining fun

Cons

  • Creepers & falling can ruin zen moments
  • Survival mode is peaceful… until you forget torches
  • Accidentally hit a villager and now you're public enemy #1
  • One misstep in lava and it's ✨rage quit✨ o'clock
