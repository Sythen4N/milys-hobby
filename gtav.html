GTA V
GO BACK GO HOME
ME
My Review
★★★★☆
44 hrs played
GTA V is that one game where I promise myself “just one mission” and end up getting distracted by punching pedestrians, stealing a bicycle, and launching it off Mount Chiliad. It’s peaceful... until I sneeze and run over five people. Honestly? Peak therapy.

Pros

  • The map is huge and beautiful where you can hike, swim, rob banks, vibe
  • You can obey traffic laws… or throw a grenade at the golf club
  • Infinite side activities: heists, races, flying jetpacks
  • Seamless shift between story and online mode

Cons

  • GTA Online = where your car gets blown up before you even log in
  • Game is 10+ years old but still refuses to retire gracefully
  • Random NPCs kill the vibe (I am NOT a speed bump, Karen!)
  • Police chases get old... and expensive
