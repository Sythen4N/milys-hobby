My Review
★★★★☆
47.9 hrs played
Valorant feels like a calm sunrise... until someone blinds you and snipes your head. It’s polished and thoughtful agents glide softly across sleek maps but once the Spike drops, it's chaos with purpose. I chill with its smooth visuals and satisfying gunplay... until I get one tapped and remember I'm not ready for esports. Still, logging off hurt? That’s a good sign.

Pros

  • Clean, minimal style = zen shooter vibes
  • Sounds are crisp and satisfying (until it’s your skull cracking)
  • Strategic gameplay that’s rewarding (when your teammates listen 😌)
  • Riot keeps things fresh with updates and agent drops

Cons

  • Ranked can ruin friendships and sleep schedules
  • One tap = heartbreak
  • Vanguard (the anti-cheat) is a bit… clingy
  • Not the best if you just wanna vibe (this game wants your soul)
