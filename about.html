BIO

Hello! I'm Normilyssa Asyira

I'm currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Media Communication Technology at UiTM Alor Gajah, Malacca. I have a strong interest in technology and enjoy working on creative projects, especially in areas like web development, design, and problem-solving. I'm always eager to learn new things, improve my skills, and take on challenges that help me grow both personally and professionally.

This website was created as part of my NMC302 assignment, guided by Sir Khairi bin Khairuddin. The main focus of this project is to share one of my biggest hobbies, which is gaming. Through this platform, I'll be reviewing my top 5 favorite games, highlighting what makes them stand out, and what they personally mean to me as a gamer.

Outside of academics, I enjoy diving into immersive game worlds, exploring new digital tools, and drawing inspiration from everyday experiences to fuel my creativity.
